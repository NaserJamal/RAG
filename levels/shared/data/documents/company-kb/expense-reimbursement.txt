TechCorp Expense Reimbursement Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Purpose
This policy establishes guidelines for business-related expenses and the reimbursement process to ensure fair, consistent, and timely payment for legitimate business costs incurred by employees.

Eligible Expenses

Business Travel
Airfare: Economy class for domestic flights; premium economy for international flights over 6 hours
Ground Transportation: Reasonable taxi, rideshare, rental car, or public transportation costs
Lodging: Up to $200/night for standard business travel; up to $300/night for high-cost metropolitan areas (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo)
Meals: Up to $75/day for domestic travel; up to $100/day for international travel

Client Entertainment
Business meals with clients or prospects: Up to $150 per person
Event tickets: Up to $300 per person when hosting clients
Entertainment venues: Reasonable costs for business development purposes
Alcohol is permitted in moderation when entertaining clients

Office Supplies
Standard office supplies up to $50/month without pre-approval
Computer accessories and peripherals up to $200 with manager approval
Standing desks, ergonomic chairs require HR approval

Professional Development
Conference registration fees up to $2,000 per event
Professional membership dues up to $500/year
Books and online courses up to $500/year
Certifications relevant to role (case-by-case approval required)

Home Office Equipment
For remote employees:
- Monitor (up to $500)
- Keyboard and mouse (up to $150)
- Desk and chair (up to $800 combined)
- High-speed internet reimbursement: $75/month

Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses will NOT be reimbursed:
- Personal entertainment or leisure activities
- Minibar charges and pay-per-view movies
- Spa treatments, gym fees, personal grooming
- Traffic violations, parking tickets
- Expenses for spouse/family members unless pre-approved for company events
- First-class or business-class upgrades on domestic flights
- Loss or theft of personal items
- Pet care or personal services

Submission Process

Timing
All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred. Expenses older than 30 days will not be reimbursed without VP approval.

Required Documentation
- Original itemized receipts (photos acceptable via mobile app)
- Credit card statements alone are NOT sufficient
- Business purpose and attendees for meals/entertainment
- For international expenses, include currency conversion details

Submission Methods
1. Web Portal: Upload receipts and complete expense form at expenses.techcorp.com
2. Mobile App: TechCorp ExpenseTracker (iOS/Android)
3. Email: expenses@techcorp.com (for technical issues only)

Approval Workflow
- Expenses under $500: Direct manager approval
- Expenses $500-$2,000: Manager + Director approval
- Expenses over $2,000: Manager + VP approval
- All international travel: Pre-approval required

Payment Timeline
Approved expenses are reimbursed via direct deposit within 10 business days of approval. Ensure your banking information is current in the HR Portal.

Corporate Credit Cards

Eligibility
Employees in the following roles are eligible for corporate credit cards:
- Directors and above
- Sales team members
- Employees with regular travel requirements (5+ trips/year)

Responsibilities
- Corporate cards should be used ONLY for business expenses
- Personal charges are strictly prohibited
- Submit expense reports monthly even when using corporate card
- Lost or stolen cards must be reported immediately to Finance

Credit Limits
- Individual contributors: $5,000/month
- Managers: $10,000/month
- Directors: $25,000/month
- VPs and above: $50,000/month

Travel Advances

For international travel or extended trips, employees may request a travel advance:
- Up to $2,000 for trips over 5 days
- Must be requested 2 weeks before travel
- Expense report due within 1 week of return
- Unused advance must be returned

Per Diem Rates (Alternative to Receipt Submission)

Employees may opt for per diem rates instead of submitting meal receipts:
- Domestic travel: $65/day
- International travel: $85/day
- Per diem covers all meals and incidentals
- Cannot combine per diem with receipt-based meal reimbursement

Mileage Reimbursement

Personal vehicle use for business:
- Standard rate: $0.67 per mile (matches IRS rate)
- Submit mileage log with start/end locations and business purpose
- Commute miles to primary office are not reimbursable
- Parking fees and tolls reimbursed separately with receipts

Policy Violations

First offense: Written warning
Second offense: Suspension of reimbursement privileges for 90 days
Third offense: Disciplinary action up to and including termination

Fraudulent expense claims will result in immediate termination and may involve legal action.
